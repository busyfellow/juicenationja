Our Story

From a kitchen blender to Kingston's shelves — the FORGE journey.

FORGE was born out of a simple idea — Jamaicans deserve a meal replacement that's made with real ingredients, by real people, right here at home.

Founder Photo

Meet the Founder

FORGE started with one person's refusal to settle. Our founder — a passionate Jamaican entrepreneur — looked at the local market and saw shelf after shelf of imported, artificially flavoured meal replacement shakes. Powder mixes packed with additives and shipped from thousands of miles away. None of them reflected what Jamaica is — a land of vibrant, real, tropical fruit.

They believed Jamaica could make something better. Something that used real banana, real strawberry, real blueberry, and real peanut. Something produced right here in Kingston, supporting local jobs and local agriculture. So they started blending — literally — in a kitchen, perfecting recipes one bottle at a time.

That obsession with quality and authenticity is what drives every decision at Juice Nation JA today.

"Every bottle of FORGE represents our commitment to real nutrition, real ingredients, and real Jamaican pride."

Our Journey

2024

The Idea

Research begins into Jamaica's meal replacement market. Identified gap: no locally-made, fruit-based options available to consumers.

2025

Recipe Development

Months of testing recipes using real fruits — banana, strawberry, blueberry, peanut. Partnered with food scientists to perfect the formula for taste, texture, and nutritional balance.

2025

First Production

First batch produced. FORGE Meal Replacement Shake officially launched with 4 flavours — marking a first for Jamaica's nutrition market.

2026

Kingston Launch

FORGE hits shelves across Kingston and St. Andrew. The response is immediate — people want real nutrition made by Jamaicans, for Jamaicans.

2026

Growing Strong

Expanding distribution across Jamaica. Targeting 75 retail locations by end of year, bringing real-fruit nutrition to every parish.
