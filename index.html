FAQ

Got Questions?

What is FORGE?

FORGE is a ready-to-drink meal replacement shake made with real fruits, soy protein, and essential vitamins and minerals. Each 500ml bottle delivers 15–20g of protein along with calcium, B12, vitamin D, iron, and potassium — designed to fuel your body when you need it most.

What flavors are available?

FORGE is available in four flavors: Blueberry Strawberry, Coffee Banana, Peanut Banana, and Peanut Banana Strawberry. Each one is crafted with real fruit ingredients for genuine flavor.

How much protein does FORGE contain?

FORGE contains between 15 and 20 grams of soy protein per 500ml serving, depending on the flavor. Peanut Banana Strawberry leads with 20g, while Blueberry Strawberry and Coffee Banana each provide 15g.

Is FORGE suitable for people with allergies?

FORGE contains peanuts and soy. It is manufactured in a facility that also processes dairy and tree nuts. If you have a known allergy to any of these ingredients, please consult your healthcare provider before consuming FORGE. Full allergen information is available on each product label.
